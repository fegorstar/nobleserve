Nobleserve Promissory Notes

Simple fixed deposit instruments which provides clients with the opportunity to earn a fixed return on investment.

Benefits

  • Minimum tenor to invest for is 90days and maximum is 365 days.
  • Minimum investible amount is 1,000,000 and you can earn up to 14%.
  • Flexibility: You can top up as you desire on or before maturity on an existing investment.

Eligibility

From 18 years and above.

  • A valid government issued ID card.
  • A recent utility bill not more than 3 months for proof of address.
  • Two passport photographs.

At maturity, a pay out of the total invested principal plus accrued interest will be paid to the client. To liquidate before the maturity date, 30% will be charged on the accrued interest of the investment. To get started, an application form will be filled and submitted with required supporting documents listed above to complete the onboarding process.

Nobleserve Target Savings Plan

The Nobleserve Target Savings Plan

Save up funds to achieve your financial goals. The tenor for the Savings Plan is 365 days.

Benefits

  • Minimum monthly savings of NGN 50,000
  • Flexibility: You can top up as you desire on a monthly basis.
  • Interest: An annual interest rate of 12% is payable and it is subject to 10% Withholding Tax.

Eligibility

From 18 years and above.

  • A valid government issued ID card.
  • A recent utility bill not more than 3 months for proof of address.
  • Two passport photographs.

The plans allows you save minimum of NGN 50,000 on a monthly basis for a period of one year. At maturity, a pay out of the total invested principal plus accrued interest of 12% will be paid to the client. To liquidate before the maturity date, 30% will be charged on the accrued interest of the investment.

To get started, an application form will be filled and submitted with required supporting documents listed above to complete the onboarding process.

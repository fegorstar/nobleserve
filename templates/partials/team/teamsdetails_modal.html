Oluwatosin Ilesanmi - Managing Director/CEO

Oluwatosin Ilesanmi is the MD/CEO of Nobleserve Finance Company Limited with over a decade of experience in Business Development, Treasury and Funds Management. She has raised and disbursed investment funds from both domestic and global clients on a broad range of transactions across various sectors including Oil & Gas, Financial Institutions, Insurance, and General Trading. She rose from the position of a General Manager to an Executive Director before becoming the MD/CEO of the Company.
Prior to joining Nobleserve Finance Company Limited in 2019; Oluwatosin was previously an Executive Director and Chief Operating Officer (COO) at Alpha Morgan Capital Managers Limited. At Alpha Morgan, she carried out the review and implementation of a business/marketing plan for liability generation from UHNIs, HNIs and Mass Affluent Individuals. She also contributed to the review of existing product programs and the development of new products to suit the lifestyle of all customers.
Prior to joining Alpha Morgan, Oluwatosin Ilesanmi worked with Greenwich Trust Limited as the Head, Registrars & Business Development Executive. She participated in marketing Stock broking services to Retail & High Net Worth individual clients, Institutional investors and management of the portfolios of High-Net-Worth individuals. Before joining Greenwich Trust Limited, Oluwatosin Ilesanmi was a Business Development Officer with GTI Capital Limited where she revamped a bulk of old and dormant client accounts to become profitable.
Oluwatosin Ilesanmi has significant experience in Asset and Funds Management, advising clients on an array of investment opportunities across a variety of investment outlets. She has managed a portfolio size of over N5 billion for Corporate Institutions, High Net worth Individuals (HNIs) and Mass Affluent Individuals. She has promoted customer acquisition, retention, and development through effective relationship management.

Oluwatoyin Aghomi - Head, Human Capital & Administration

Oluwatoyin is a versatile, excellence-driven, and seasoned HR professional with over a decade of work experience spanning core Human Resources and Customer Service in the Aviation and Finance sectors. Oluwatoyin commenced her career as a Customer Service Agent at Arik Air Limited where she passionately provided excellent customer service to the airline passengers by disseminating quality information about the airline's products and services. In pursuit of her goal to add value to the HR industry and having successfully bagged an ACIPM qualification, she was redeployed to the Human Resources department as Human Resources Advisor. In addition to the broad range of HR functions covering talent acquisition, HR initiatives, health and safety, learning and development, industrial relations, performance management, etc., she supervised the staff redundancy projects in Cote d Ivoire, Sierra Leone, and the Gambia while ensuring consistency with policies and the applicable local laws. After 2 years of meritorious service, she was promoted to Human Resources Supervisor where she was committed to adding value to people and processes through effective HR management initiatives, programs, and practices.
At Nobleserve Finance Company Limited, she functions as the Head of Human Capital and Administration where she’s responsible for implementing and monitoring the overall HR strategies, systems, policies, and procedures across the organization. She supports current and future business needs through the development, engagement, motivation, and retention of human capital. Oluwatoyin also oversees the company’s day-to-day administrative processes and efforts to ensure the judicious deployment of resources as well as maintain a safe and secure work environment while monitoring costs and expenses.
Oluwatoyin is highly resourceful, ethical, and possesses excellent people management skills. She’s passionate about developing talents to their full potential and committed to helping businesses achieve strategic goals and objectives through people.
